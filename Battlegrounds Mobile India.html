3. Battlegrounds Mobile India

Next on the list is Krafton launched Battlegrounds Mobile India (BGMI) in India as a rebadged PUBG Mobile and the game has garnered close to 50 million downloads within a month. This is also a battle royale game that allows players to jump into an amazing array of maps, game modes, and exciting challenges. The game drops 100 players on a map to fight against each other complete mission and stay till the last circle to win the match. BGMI is one of the most played Android games, and it will soon be available on iOS as well.
